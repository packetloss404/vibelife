VibeLife Plugin Guide

Everything you can do on a VibeLife server. Commands, keybinds, features, and all the fun stuff waiting for you.

🎮

What is VibeLife?

VibeLife adds social features, an economy, land ownership, achievements, pets, seasons, and much more to your Minecraft experience. It turns a regular server into a living world with things to do, stuff to earn, and people to meet.

When you join a VibeLife server, you're automatically set up — no registration needed. Just hop in and start playing. Your balance, friends, parcels, and achievements are all tracked from the moment you arrive.
📝

Commands

These are all the commands available to you as a player. Type them in chat to use them.

Land & Parcels

/parcel info
See info about the land you're standing on — who owns it, whether it's public, and who can build.
/parcel claim
Claim the unclaimed land you're standing on as yours. You become the owner and can control who builds there.
/parcel release
Give up your claimed land. It becomes unclaimed and available for someone else.
/parcel list
See all parcels in the current region — who owns what and where.
Aliases: /plot, /land

Friends & Social

/friends list
See your current friends and any pending friend requests waiting for you.
/friends add <player>
Send a friend request to another player. They'll get a notification.
/friends remove <player>
Remove someone from your friends list.
/friends messages
Read messages people sent you while you were offline.
Alias: /friends msg

Economy

/balance
Check how many Vibes (currency) you currently have.
/pay <player> <amount>
Send Vibes to another player. Great for buying items or tipping builders.
/market
Browse what other players are selling on the marketplace.

Economy

The VibeLife economy runs on Vibes — the universal currency across the entire platform.

How Vibes Work

You earn Vibes by playing — completing achievements, participating in events, selling items on the marketplace, and more. You can spend them just as easily:

  • Pay other players directly with /pay
  • Buy items, blueprints, and resources on the marketplace
  • Bid on auctions for rare and seasonal items
  • Tip builders whose work you admire

Vibes are shared across all VibeLife servers, so your wealth travels with you.

🏠

Your Land

The world is divided into parcels. Here's how land ownership works from your perspective as a player.

Claiming Land

Walk around and find a spot you like. Use /parcel info to check if it's unclaimed. If it is, use /parcel claim to make it yours. That's it — you now own that land.

  • Only you and collaborators you invite can build on your parcel
  • Public parcels exist where anyone can build
  • You cannot build outside of parcels — find one to claim or use a public one
  • Use /parcel release if you want to give up your land
  • Use /parcel list to see all parcels in your region

Friends & Social

VibeLife has a built-in social system so you can connect with people you meet in-game.

Staying Connected

  • Send friend requests with /friends add <player>
  • See who's online from your friends list
  • Send offline messages that players receive next time they log in
  • Join groups to organize with larger teams
  • Your online status is visible to your friends so they know when you're around
🏆

Achievements

Just play the game and achievements unlock automatically. No need to activate or track them — the system watches what you do.

Things That Earn Achievements

  • Building — Place blocks, complete structures, use blueprints
  • Mining — Break blocks, find ores, dig deep
  • Combat — Kill mobs, defeat bosses, survive close calls
  • Exploration — Visit new regions, discover landmarks, travel far
  • Social — Chat with players, make friends, join guilds

When you unlock an achievement, you'll get a notification on screen. Some achievements grant titles you can equip, and your progress shows on leaderboards.

Events

Throughout the year, servers host special events that anyone can join:

  • Build Competitions — Themed building challenges with prizes
  • Dance Parties — Emote sync events with bonus XP
  • Concerts — In-world music events at special venues
  • Market Days — Boosted marketplace activity with special deals

Check the events screen (press K with the Fabric mod) to see what's coming up and RSVP.
