VibeLife Game Manual

The complete guide to exploring, building, battling, and socializing in VibeLife — a Minecraft Paper server plugin with an optional Fabric client mod.

What is VibeLife?

VibeLife is a Paper server plugin that transforms any Minecraft server into a social MMORPG. It adds land ownership with parcels, a full player economy powered by Vibes currency, RPG combat with stats and leveling, friends and guilds, achievements, pets, seasonal content, events, and much more. Players join a Minecraft server running the VibeLife plugin and are automatically set up — no separate registration needed. Your Minecraft account is your identity. The optional VibeLife Fabric client mod adds rich UI overlays for managing economy, social features, marketplace, achievements, and events.

1. Getting Started

Joining a Server

Join any Minecraft server running the VibeLife plugin. You are automatically authenticated by your Minecraft account — no separate registration, login, or account creation is needed. The moment you join, your balance, friends, parcels, and achievements begin tracking.

Automatic setup: When you first join a VibeLife server, you start with 1,000 Vibes (currency). Your inventory, parcels, friends list, and progress are all saved and persist between sessions.

If you have the optional Fabric client mod installed, you get extra UI screens for economy, social, marketplace, achievements, and events. Without it, everything is still accessible via chat commands.

The Fabric Client Mod

The VibeLife Fabric mod is optional but enhances your experience significantly. It adds rich GUI screens for all major features, so you can manage your economy, friends, marketplace, achievements, and events through visual interfaces instead of chat commands alone. Install it like any other Fabric mod — drop the JAR into your mods/ folder.

Tip: Everything you can do with the Fabric mod can also be done via chat commands. The mod just makes it more convenient with dedicated UI screens.

2. The World

Regions

The VibeLife world is divided into regions — discrete, named areas that players can explore. Each region has its own terrain, parcels, enemies, and atmosphere. Regions track their population in real-time and support many concurrent players.

Parcels & Land Ownership

Each region is subdivided into rectangular parcels defined by coordinate boundaries. Parcels are the foundation of the building and ownership system.

Parcel Tiers

TierDescriptionBuilding Access
PublicCommunity land, open to allAnyone can build
ResidentialClaimable by any playerOwner and collaborators only
PremiumPremium claimable landOwner and collaborators only

Claiming Land

To claim an unclaimed residential or premium parcel, stand on it and type /parcel claim. Once claimed, only you and any collaborators you add can build on that parcel. Use /parcel release to release parcels you no longer need. Use /parcel info to see who owns the land you're standing on.

Collaborators

Parcel owners can invite other players as collaborators, granting them full building rights on that parcel. This enables group building projects.

Navigation & Teleportation

Move through the world using standard Minecraft movement. The plugin adds waypoint-based teleportation on top:

Teleport Points

  • Create waypoint — saves your current position with a custom name
  • List waypoints — view all your saved teleport points
  • Teleport — instantly travel to any saved waypoint
  • Cross-region teleport — travel between different regions; your position in the previous region is saved

Day/Night Cycle

VibeLife uses Minecraft's day/night cycle, enhanced by the plugin with seasonal atmosphere changes affecting sky tints, fog, and ambient lighting.

Seasons & Weather

3. Building

Block Building

Building in VibeLife is standard Minecraft block building. Place blocks, break blocks, craft items — the full Minecraft experience. The VibeLife plugin controls where you can build through the parcel system.

Tip: You can only build on parcels where you have permission — public parcels, parcels you own, or parcels where you're a collaborator. Use /parcel info to check permissions for the land you're standing on.

How It Works

  • On your parcel, you can freely place and break blocks
  • On public parcels, anyone can build
  • On a collaborator's parcel, you have full building rights if they added you
  • On unclaimed land, you cannot build — claim it first with /parcel claim
  • On another player's parcel, building is denied (a message shows the owner's name)

Build Permissions

Permission Rules

  • Public parcels — Everyone can build freely
  • Your parcel — Full control over your claimed land
  • Collaborator — Full build rights if the owner added you
  • Unclaimed parcel — Cannot build; claim it first
  • Other player's parcel — Denied (message shows owner name)

Blueprints

Save your creations as blueprints — reusable templates that store the full arrangement of blocks. Blueprints can be:

  • Saved from your current build
  • Loaded from your blueprint library
  • Placed to instantly recreate the entire arrangement on your parcel
  • Sold on the marketplace for other players to purchase

4. Combat & RPG

Combat in VibeLife uses standard Minecraft combat mechanics — melee hits, ranged attacks, blocking. The plugin layers an RPG system on top, tracking stats, XP, levels, loot drops, and more.

Player Stats

Every player has RPG combat stats tracked by the plugin that scale with their combat level:

Stats at a Glance

HP (Health Points) = 100 + level x 20

Mana = 50 + level x 10

XP to Next Level = level x 100

Regeneration

Health and mana regenerate every second while alive:

  • HP Regen — 2% of Max HP per second
  • Mana Regen — 3% of Max Mana per second

Attacking

Combat uses standard Minecraft attack mechanics. Hit mobs with your weapon or use ranged attacks. The plugin tracks two damage styles on top of normal Minecraft combat:

Melee Attack

Damage = Strength x 1.0 − Enemy Defense
Mana Cost: None
Best for: Sustained combat, no resource cost

Magic Attack

Damage = Strength x 1.5 − Enemy Defense
Mana Cost: 20 per cast
Best for: Burst damage, 50% stronger than melee

Critical Hits: Every attack has a 10% chance to deal double damage. Critical hits are announced in chat.

Minimum damage is always 1, even if the enemy's defense exceeds your strength.

Enemies

The plugin spawns custom enemies in regions. These mobs have AI with multiple behavior states and are tracked by the RPG system:

VariantHPSTRDEFDifficulty
Slime5052Easy
Skeleton80105Medium
Golem2001515Hard (tanky)
Shadow100203Hard (glass cannon)
Drake5002520Boss

Enemy stats scale with their level: HP increases by 25% per level, Strength and Defense by 20% per level. Enemies can spawn at levels 1–5.

Enemy AI Behavior

  1. Idle — Standing still, scanning for players within 8 blocks
  2. Patrol — Wandering randomly within 5 blocks of spawn (20% chance per tick)
  3. Aggro — Player detected! Locks on to nearest target
  4. Chase — Moves toward player
  5. Attack — Within melee range, attacks every 1.5 seconds
  6. Dead — Defeated; respawns after 30 seconds at original spawn point

Enemies give up the chase if the player moves more than 15 blocks from the enemy's spawn point.

Death & Respawn

When your HP reaches zero:

  1. You die and see the standard Minecraft death screen
  2. 5% of your Vibes are deducted as a death penalty
  3. Your RPG stats (HP and Mana) are fully restored on respawn
  4. You respawn at your bed or world spawn as normal
  5. Your death counter increases
Warning: The death penalty can be significant at high wealth levels. Bank your Vibes by investing in marketplace items if you're heading into dangerous territory.

Leveling & XP

Defeating enemies awards 20 x enemy level XP. When your accumulated XP reaches the threshold, you level up automatically:

  • All stats are recalculated to new level values
  • HP and Mana are fully restored
  • Excess XP carries over to the next level
  • A "Level Up!" notification appears in chat
  • Maximum combat level: 100

5. Social Features

Chat

VibeLife uses Minecraft's chat system with additional features layered by the plugin:

Region Chat

Standard Minecraft chat broadcasts to everyone in your region. Messages are limited to 180 characters.

Whisper (Private Messages)

Type /w <username> <message> to send a private message to another player. Whispers are only visible to sender and recipient.

Group Chat

Members of a group (guild) can chat in their group channel. Messages are visible to all group members regardless of which region they're in.

Chat history is buffered (last 50 messages per region) and sent to you when you join a region.

Friends

Build your social network with the friends system:

  • /friends add <player> — Send a friend request
  • /friends list — See friends and pending requests
  • /friends remove <player> — Remove a friend
  • Block — Block a player to prevent them from whispering or interacting with you
  • Unblock — Remove a block

Your friends list shows each friend's online status, current region, and custom status message.

Offline Messages

Send messages (up to 1,000 characters) to friends who are offline. They'll see your messages when they next log in via /friends messages.

Groups & Guilds

Emote Combos

When two players perform the same compatible emote within 3 seconds and 3 blocks of each other, a special combo effect triggers:

  • High-Five — celebratory burst
  • Dance — synchronized dance effect
  • Bow — respectful exchange
  • Wave — friendly greeting effect

Voice Chat

VibeLife supports spatial voice chat within regions:

  • Join/Leave — Connect to your region's voice channel (max 32 participants)
  • Mute — Stop transmitting your voice
  • Deafen — Mute all incoming audio
  • Volume — Adjust from 0.0 to 1.0

Spatial Audio

Voice volume scales based on distance. Players beyond 30 blocks are silent. This creates natural proximity-based conversations.

Presence & Status

Set your online status to communicate availability:

  • Online — Actively playing (default)
  • Busy — In a focused activity
  • Away — Auto-set after 10 minutes of inactivity
  • Invisible — Appear offline to others
  • Offline — Logged out

You can also set a custom status message (e.g., "Building my castle!" or "AFK for dinner").

6. Economy

Currency

VibeLife's economy runs on Vibes — the universal in-game currency. New players start with 1,000 Vibes. Check your balance any time with /balance.

Earning Vibes

  • Combat loot — Defeat enemies for guaranteed Vibes drops
  • Marketplace sales — Sell items to other players
  • Achievements & challenges — XP and Vibes rewards
  • Quest rewards — NPC quest completion
  • Gifts — Receive from other players via /pay

Spending Vibes

  • Marketplace purchases — Buy items, blueprints, and resources
  • Pay players — Send Vibes with /pay <player> <amount>
  • Guild treasury — Deposit into your guild's bank
  • Death penalty — Lose 5% on death in combat

All transactions are logged with type, amount, and timestamp for full audit trail.

Marketplace

The marketplace is VibeLife's player-driven economy hub. Access it via /market or press M with the Fabric mod:

  • List items — Set a fixed price or create an auction
  • Browse — Filter by item kind, price range, and sort order
  • Buy — Purchase fixed-price items instantly
  • Price History — View the last 50 sales for any item to track market trends
  • Cancel — Remove your active listings

Listable Item Types

Outfit, Accessory, Tool, Pet, Decoration, Consumable, Blueprint

Trading

Direct player-to-player trading with the trade offer system:

  1. Create a trade offer specifying items and/or Vibes you're offering
  2. Specify what items and/or Vibes you want in return
  3. The recipient can accept, decline, or counter
  4. On acceptance, items and Vibes are exchanged atomically

Auctions

List items for competitive bidding:

  • Set a minimum bid amount
  • Auctions run for a set duration (default: 24 hours)
  • Bid Vibes are held (unavailable for other purchases) until you're outbid
  • When outbid, your held Vibes are automatically released
  • Highest bidder wins when the auction expires

Mobile Companion App

Stay connected to VibeLife on the go with the mobile companion:

  • Dashboard — Quick stats, online friends, recent activity
  • Inventory Management — View and manage your items
  • Marketplace Browsing — Browse and purchase listings
  • Friends List — See who's online and their current region

Federation (Multi-Server)

VibeLife supports federation — multiple servers can interconnect to form a larger world:

  • Health Monitoring — Heartbeat system with 5-minute timeout; servers marked online, offline, or degraded
